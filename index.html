About Me

From the time I was a child, I knew I wanted to be in software development. I remember playing computer games when I was young, peeking behind the monitor trying to figure out how characters like Carmen San Diego and the Little Mermaid were appearing on my screen. My fascination progressed and in the early years of the social media boom, I could spend hours creating and customizing blog/Xanga skins, implementing the new features on MySpace, and looking for new technology to get my hands on.

Now with 5 years of experience in software startups under my belt, I offer a competitive edge in the job market. In addition to my technical skills, I have marketed both B2C and B2B applications, worked cross-departmentally with Agile/SCRUM teams, and have developed the ability to manage heavy workloads in fast paced environments, pivoting at the drop of the hat.
