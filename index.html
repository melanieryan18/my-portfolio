About Me

From the time I was five or six years old, I knew I wanted to be in technology and development. I remember playing computer games in my parent's basement as a child, looking behind the monitor trying to figure out how characters like Carmen San Diego and the Little Mermaid were appearing on my screen. I was completely addicted to the games and in awe of the technology (and frankly credit my quick typing speed to all those hours on Mavis Beacon!). In the early years of the social media boom, I could spend hours creating and customizing blog/Xanga skins, implementing the new features on MySpace, and looking for new technology to get my hands on.

Now with 5 years of experience in software start ups under my belt, a certification in Full Stack Development, Front End Development, and Visual Design (in progress), I offer a competitive edge in the job market. Having marketed both B2C and B2B applications, I know what an end user wants - and how to develop it. I have also managed User Acceptance Testing (etc), written technical documentation, managed technical community sites, audited websites for million dollar companies, and so much more.

I am currently seeing opportunities in Software Engineering & Development, and am open to freelance opportunities.
