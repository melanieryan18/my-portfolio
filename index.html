About Me

I've dreamed of pursuing software development since childhood - technology has always fascinated me. I remember playing computer games when I was young, peeking behind the monitor trying to figure out how characters like Carmen San Diego were appearing on my screen.

Now, with 5 years of experience in software startups under my belt, I offer a competitive edge in the job market. In addition to my technical skills, I have marketed both B2C and B2B applications, worked cross-departmentally with Agile/SCRUM teams, audited websites for million dollar businesses, and have a taste of every phase of the development lifecycle from a non-technical standpoint. I am deeply organized, highly communicative, and manage my time with ease.

I currently work as a Full Stack Developer at a global biotech company, working cross-departmentally to determine requirements and develop apps for internal teams. I currently utilize Vue and vanilla JavaScript, BootStrap CSS, Axios, jQuery, PHP, SQL, and MySQL. Next on my agenda is an AWS Cloud Practioner Certification.

I am always open to learning about new opportunities and pro bono nonprofit opportunities. Click the links below to connect on LinkedIn, view my GitHub, and get in touch via email.
